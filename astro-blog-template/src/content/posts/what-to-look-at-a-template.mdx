## Not Everything That Looks Good Is Useful

A good design doesn't guarantee a good base project.

---

## Positive Signs

- Clear structure
- Minimal gimmicks
- Easy to modify
- Good documentation

---

## Red Flags

- Logic mixed with content
- Unnecessary dependencies
- Over-engineering

---

## Conclusion

A template should save you time, not tie you to other people's decisions.
